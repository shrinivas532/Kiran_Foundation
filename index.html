Our Vision

Building a self-reliant generation through knowledge, skills, and opportunity.

Who We Are

KIRAN Foundation (Knowledge, Innovation, Reform, Aspiration, Nurture) is a grassroots initiative based in Kudachi, Raibag Taluk, Belagavi District, Karnataka. We work directly with rural youth — many of whom are first-generation learners — to bridge the gap between education and employability.

Our mission: Every young person in our community deserves a path to dignified livelihood — regardless of their background.

Our Programs

Comprehensive training pathways tailored for rural youth.

💻

Digital Literacy

Computer basics, internet skills, smartphone usage, and digital safety — building confidence in the digital world.

🗣️

Communication Skills

Spoken English, interview preparation, public speaking, and professional communication for workplace readiness.

🔧

Vocational Training

Hands-on training in trades like tailoring, electrical work, agriculture tech, and small business management.

📋

Government Scheme Guidance

Helping youth navigate and access government employment programs, scholarships, and welfare schemes they're entitled to.

🤝

Mentorship & Placement

One-on-one mentoring, career counseling, resume building, and direct connections to local and regional employers.

👩‍💼

Women's Empowerment

Dedicated programs for young women — self-help groups, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship bootcamps.
